XBOX Controller CAD Modeling

Comprehensive 3D modeling project showcasing precision in SolidWorks CAD design and photorealistic rendering in KeyShot. This project demonstrates attention to detail in recreating complex consumer electronics through technical modeling and advanced visualization techniques.

Smart Cane

An innovative assistive device designed to enhance mobility for visually impaired users. Integrating Arduino technology with distance sensors to detect obstacles at height level, providing auditory feedback for safer navigation.

Technology Integration

The Smart Cane combines ergonomic design principles with advanced sensor technology. The Arduino-based system processes distance measurements to alert users of overhead obstacles, filling a critical gap in traditional mobility aids.

BiBike

A revolutionary dual-mode bicycle design that seamlessly transitions between regular outdoor cycling and stationary indoor exercise. Perfect for maintaining fitness routines regardless of weather conditions or space constraints.

ModuTable

A thoughtfully designed modular organization system created specifically for my grandmother's kitchen table. Each component addresses her daily activities while maintaining order and accessibility in a compact space.

Personalized Design

This modular table set transforms chaos into calm by providing dedicated spaces for everything from meal preparation to crafting. The system adapts to different activities while keeping essentials organized and within reach.

AirSip

A humanitarian design solution for water-scarce regions in Africa. This atmospheric water harvester uses condensation technology to extract clean drinking water from ambient air, providing a sustainable water source for communities in need.
